Located on the east coast of the United States of America, Jersey City is the second-most populous city in the state of New Jersey and the largest of its county 'Hudson County'. While it is located just across the New York Upper Bay and Hudson River from New York City and part of the Tri-state area, Jersey City has it's very own character and vibe. Jersey City is known to be the center for banking and finance business, it's redeveloped waterfront even carries the nickname 'Wall Street West'. Jersey City's has a large public transportation system with great connections to Manhattan, beautiful parks and recreation areas as well as a lot of history. This makes this city become not only the residence of choice for a diverse population but also a global destination for visitors. Ongoing development also caused Jersey City to be the City with the highest median rental rates in the United States. More information and insightful data on the cities history, recent changes, future development plans and visions can be found in the 'Jersey City Master Plan Vision' and on the Website of the Devision of City Planning.
